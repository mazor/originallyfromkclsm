Welcome to module selection for third year students doing Computer Science Bcs or Computer Science with Management Bcs. The purpose of this webpage is for you to select which modules you wish to do for your final year. Check out the drop-down buttons to view and explore the options available to you. If you are doing Computer Science Bcs you will have to choose three optional modules for each term, three for term one, and another three for term two. In addition to the optional modules, all third year students must do the Individual Project. This module is for both terms, and is worth 30 credits. If you are doing Computer Science with Management Bcs you have two compulsory modules. During first term you will have to do Business Strategy. During second term you will do Human Resource Management. Because of the compulsory modules, management students will choose two optional modules for each term instead of three.
The modules all belong under one of these areas; Performance Critical Computing, Internet Systems, Software Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence. If you wish to delelop expertise in one of these areas, we recommend that you choose the most relevant modules. When you select a module to explore in the drop-down box, you will be informed which of these areas the module in recommended for. If you are not interested in focusing on one area, you can choose your modules from different areas.
